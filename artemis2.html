NASA · Artemis II · Mission Complete
View From Orion

Artemis II flew April 1–10, 2026, humanity's first crewed journey to lunar distance since Apollo 17. This is a complete historical replay of the mission, reconstructed from real NASA telemetry.

The Earth, Moon, Sun, and stars are placed using real orbital mechanics at each moment of the flight. Drag to look around. Use the playback bar to scrub through the full mission timeline.

The spacecraft itself is omitted. The experience is meant to mimic going up to the window on the Orion capsule and watching the universe move past.
