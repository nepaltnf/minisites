View From Orion

This is a simulation of the live view from the Orion spacecraft (within 60 seconds). Position and velocity are supplied by NASA AROW telemetry. The Earth, Stars, Moon, Sun, and planets are placed using real orbital mechanics.

Drag to look around. Select a camera lens to zoom. The Eye view is set to a 25mm equivalent lens, to simulate the focal frame of the window.

The spacecraft itself is omitted. The experience is meant to mimic going up to the window on the Orion Spacecraft.
